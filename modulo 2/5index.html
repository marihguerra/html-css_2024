Grouping tags in HTML5

HTML5's focus is centered in semantics, so are its grouping tags. They are a group of tags that work together to create the website's structure, organizing it in a semantic way. These tags don't add or change the content in any way since they work like div's.

<header> An introductory content;   

<nav> Set of navigation links;   

<footer> Usually a message, extra info;   

<main> Main content, unique to the website;   

<section> A piece of content that can stand on its own;   

<article> A container for self contained text;   

<aside> Peripheral content related to the article;
