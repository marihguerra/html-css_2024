Playing audios

We're gonna learn how to play audios in HTML5. In the audio tag, you can put attributes like "auto" and "autoplay" for the audio to play as soon as you enter the website, "loop" to play it on loop, "metadata" and "controls" so you can control the audiofile and if you don't want it all to be downloaded right away, only the main data.
